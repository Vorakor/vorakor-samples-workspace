Ngx Health Checker

Ngx Health Checker is your IT managers dream, it informs you and your application when things go awry and puts up a neat window informing your users that we're going to keep trying until everything goes right again.

Ngx Health Checker features an optional component with pre-built modals, but the real gem of Ngx Health Checker is the service. This beast of a service has a number of built in features that can be configured to suit your applications and your needs, including servers and endpoints configuration so that Ngx Health Checker can ping your server for you and tell you when it can't reach it, it also features a debug setting that will log to the console to show you what the Ngx Health Checker is up to.

In addition to this, Ngx Health Checker comes configured to handle your needs with http errors, including an observable to track errors that come back from your server and will in the future inclue a piece that can be put in as a global error handler so you can track those pesky application problems. Additionally, Ngx Health Checker comes built with a web socket so that your server can communicate with you and let you know what is going on in the black box.
